About Me...

Education

Thompson High School: I went to highschool at Thompson High School in Alabaster, AL, where I was the captain of the tennis team as well as a member of the National Honor Society, Spanish Honor Society, and the Math Honor Society. I was also the student council vice president and was the salutatorian of my graduating class in 2013.

Jacksonville State University: I attended JSU, in Jacksonville, AL, from 2013 to 2015. Before transferring, I was a student government representative and was on the Dean's list my freshman year.

Unversity of Alabama at Birmingham: I attend UAB, in Birmingham, AL, from 2015 until I graduated in 2019. I transferred to UAB to be study business while working to forward my career at Publix. While working hard to complete my bachelor's degree in General Studies with minors in marketing and business administration. I also completed a butcher's apprenticeship at Publix and went on to become an assistant department manager prior to graduating.

Interests

I take care of them in all of the obvious ways, but they seem to know when I need to be distracted or when I need to make myself take a break and just go for a walk. I remember getting Hank for the Greater Birmingham Human Society and we've been inseperable ever since.
